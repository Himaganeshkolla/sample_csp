Cercospora leaf spot: Cercospora canescens Symptom *Spots produced are small, numerous in number with pale brown centre and reddish brown margin. Similar spots also occur on branches and pods. *Under favourable environmental conditions, severe leaf spotting and defoliation occurs at the time of flowering and pod formation. The fungus is seed-borne and also survives on plant debris in the soil. *High humidity favours disease development. Management *Spray Carbendazim 500 g/ha or Mancozeb 1000g /ha at initiation of the disease and 10 days later.
